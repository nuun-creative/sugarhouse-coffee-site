# Sugar House Coffee — Website Redesign

A redesigned website concept for [Sugar House Coffee](https://www.sugarhousecoffee.com/) in Salt Lake City, UT.

Built with love by a regular who thinks this incredible shop deserves a web presence that matches its vibe.

## About Sugar House Coffee

Sugar House Coffee has been the heart of the Sugar House neighborhood since 2002. They're a community gathering space committed to locally sourced food, Rimini-roasted coffee, supporting local artists, and welcoming everyone.

**Enjoy coffee. Eat local. Be kind.**

## Tech

Single-file HTML/CSS/JS — no build tools, no dependencies, no frameworks. Just open `index.html`.
